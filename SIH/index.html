Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need internet all the time?

No! Our app works offline for most functions. You can conduct fitness tests and view results without internet.

What tests are included?

Vertical Jump, Shuttle Run, Endurance Run, Sit-ups, Push-ups, Flexibility Tests, and Balance Assessments.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely! We use encryption and comply with privacy standards. Your data is never shared without consent.

Can schools use it for groups?

Yes! We offer group features for schools with batch assessments and progress tracking.
